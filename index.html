Chronicles Of The Bwengo Man

Introduction

Once upon a time in a planet far far away in one of the spirals of the milky way called the Orion Arm lies a small habitable planet called Earth which literally means dirt it's funny we don't know who actually named this planet. But anyway The Bwengo Man whose full names are Muhammadi Hanif Bwengo was born on this very planet on the 20th of November 213 years after Shaka, he neither witnessed the first nor the second world war. This guy is a nerd like an Otai inter Galactic level Otaku, I personally fear for his girlfriend if he's even lucky enough to have one. Anyway below lies The Chronicles of the Bwengo Man.

Life in pre school

In the very year the Tech giant facebook was born, The Bwengo Man started his pre school. It has been reported and confirmed that the Bwengo has a natural hate for "School" but little did we know that this guy actually ran away from school on the very first day he reported for class. It's funny to know that a person who looks like they enjoy school deep down they actually hate it. It's sad to know that the Bwengo Man doesn't remember much about pre school except the part for the graduation in which he was given toys for graduating to the first grade.

Life in Primary school

Just three years before the global bank, Lehman Brothers crushed which caused global crisis The Bwengo Man had finally made it to the first grade. And by the time the Lehman Brothers crushed the Bwengo Man was in the third grade thus he barely felt the impact of the crisis but many adults at that time cried and complained as they watched the world economy go down in one of the worlds worst recessions to ever take place. The Bwengo Man completed his primary school in the year the Zambian national soccer team first won the Africa Cup Of Nations soccer Cup at a school in the streets of Lusaka called George Central Basic school in which he graduated to a school for his junior high then, called Matero Boys Basic School now commonly known as New Matero Secondary School.

Life in Junior Primary School (in poem form)

Junior Primary was fun, I recall being a lone wolf who did not belong to any clan, I was so reckless I did not have any plan. I enjoyed video games so much such that I ran away from school.
